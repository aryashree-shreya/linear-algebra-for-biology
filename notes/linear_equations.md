# Linear Equations
## Definition
A linear equation is an equation where the variables should have the power 1.
The general form of a linear equation is:
a₁x₁ + a₂x₂ + ... + aₙxₙ = b
where 
a<sub>1 , a<sub> +..... ,a<sub>n are coefficients of variable
x<sub>1 ,x<sub>2....<sub>n are variiables
b is a constant
## Characteristics
Each variable should have the power 1
Variables cannot be multiplied or appear in denominators or in square roots.
Coefficents can be any real number or complex number.
